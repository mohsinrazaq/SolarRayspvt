About Us

Solar Rays is your one stop shop for Solar based power. We are committed to protecting the environment, so that we can leave a greener legacy to the future generations. We believe here at Solar Rays, that the children are truly our future, and as such, have pledged to help the education sector by introducing an easy instalment system for schools and offices.


We are a UK based company that install solar power in commercial and residential buildings, houses, solar tube wells, solar water filtration plants, solar drip irrigation and solar fodder cutting machinery.


This rapidly emerging company utilises the latest technological advancements to cover all sectors; domestic, commercial, industrial and agricultural in its profile. We pride ourselves on being an alternative energy solution providing company, bringing all types of solar solutions for all your energy needs, with our high-quality panel system. Moreover, every product comes with our peace of mind guarantee.

So, what are you waiting for? Your affordable greener cleaner future awaits!
